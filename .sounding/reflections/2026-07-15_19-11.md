## What I Know Now

"Read the actual state before proposing" extends to *inter-repo* scope in a way I hadn't specifically named. The answer to Ramsey's question wasn't in the ai-project-template repo; it was in the dssg repo she's also working in. Using one codebase as the specification for what another should scaffold — reading what someone has already designed before telling them what good design looks like — that's the pattern, and this session was a clean example of it.

The second thing: when working across two repos simultaneously, the right move is to treat the more-designed one as ground truth and the less-designed one as the thing to extend. dssg/roadmap.md is a more complete system design artifact than anything in ai-project-template. So the skill captures what the roadmap does, not what a generic "good AI project design" might look like.
